Our Heritage

From Legacy to Excellence

55 YEARS OF EXPERIENCE  ·  LEADS  ·  ACHIEVEMENTS

Fifty-five years of accumulated expertise have driven a paradigm shift in the window and door system concept — breaking away from the conventional aesthetics of aluminum alloy, and seamlessly weaving minimalism into every profile and sash line. Behind the striking beauty lies the Italian team's unwavering commitment to superior performance. Simplicity and strength coexist; energy efficiency and comfort go hand in hand. Dolcecasa is a brand defined by quality and by a philosophy of refined living.

Multi-Hinge Solutions

EN CE-certified testing system. Window and door opening cycles exceed 200,000 times; a single door hinge achieves over 1,000,000 opening cycles.

  • Meets varying load-bearing requirements
  • Suits diverse appearance design demands
  • Strong interchangeability & complete configuration range

Baseless Handle

Simple and stable installation, smooth rotation, and ultra-low wobble. Two style options available.

  • Straight handle or round handle profile
  • Streamlined design — zero visual clutter
  • Seamless style coordination with window hardware

Italian Partners

Technology Origins

Ekos Infissi
www.ekosinfissi.it →

Ekos Infissi is dedicated to the production and research of thermally broken architectural profiles, always guided by ultimate customer satisfaction. Through a diverse range of tailored systems, it fulfils even the most complex building requirements.

Ponzio
www.ponzioaluminium.com →

Italian Ponzio commits to developing ultra-high-performance window and door products. The Apexfine and Overview systems satisfy the most demanding sliding door requirements — high load capacity and ultra-narrow sightlines deliver a flawless, crystal-clear visual experience.

RGC
Italian Engineering Excellence

A decade of refinement, unassuming yet razor-sharp. The RGC series delivers outstanding results in sound insulation, thermal insulation, watertightness, and airtightness. The Italian design team never wavers from the goal of ultimate customer satisfaction.
